LOONA

LOONA (이달의 소녀; stylized as LOOΠΔ) is an upcoming twelve-member girl group formed by BlockBerryCreative. Starting in October 2016, a member will be introduced with a solo single and MV release every month over the course of 18 months. The full-group debut is expected in the summer of 2018, after the introduction of the third subunit. They currently have 12 active members along with 2 sub-units.
